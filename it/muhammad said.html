Sea Food

Fish and other seafood tend to be very healthy and nutritious. They’re rich in omega-3 fatty acids, two nutrients in which most people are deficient.Like, Shrimp,Salmon And Sardine

Bread

Many people eat a lot of highly processed white bread. For those trying to adopt a healthier diet, it can be extremely challenging to find healthy breads. Even so, options are available.

Grains

Although grains have gotten a bad rap in recent years, some types are very healthy. Just keep in mind that they’re relatively high in carbs, Like, Rice and Oats

Dairy

For those who can tolerate them, dairy products are a healthy source of various important nutrients. Full-fat dairy seems to be the best, and studies show that people who eat the most full-fat dairy have a lower risk of obesity and type 2 diabetes (13, 14).

Chocolate

Dark chocolate is loaded with magnesium and serves as one of the planet’s most powerful sources of antioxidants (20).
